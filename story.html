Out of the Black

Before an Aethelgard blade ever meets the whetstone, it exists as a concept of pure shadow. We believe that a knife should not merely be a tool, but an extension of aesthetic intent. We source exclusively from boutique tungsten and carbon foundries.

The Unyielding Grain

Observe the microscopic perfection beneath the surface. True high-carbon Damascus steel requires atmospheric control so precise that the margin of error is virtually nonexistent. The resulting grain structure forms an incredibly dense, obsidian-like webbing.

Carbon Weave Macro

Shot at 400x magnification. Note the molecular alignment that provides our legendary tension threshold.

Each curve on our Karambit or Balisong models is computer-machined and then hand-filed over a period of 72 hours bare minimum. The result is a glass-like finish that retains the intimidating aura of raw, unpolished iron.
